Shortcut for Publishers

Taking the service in use

There are three simple steps to take Shortcut in use for your publication.

  1. 1.

    Contact us. We will connect you with your local Shortcut representative who will create a customized account for you in the Shortcut Manager so you can manage your publications. You will get trained in the processes and handling of the Shortcut Manager. Your local Shortcut representative will be always there for you.

  2. 2.

    Submit your publication in format PDF on our FTP server, where we will set up automatic import and indexing so that the publication is available in the Shortcut Manager the moment you upload it. For this, we need a special file naming convention

  3. 3.

    Communicate the Shortcut service in your publication in order that your readers understand what it is and how it helps them in their reading experience. You can track in real-time the impact of your communication in the Statistics section within the Shortcut Manager. Also, we provide you with some sample communication, which you can easily copy-paste in your layout.

What other information do we need?
  • Publication logo (min. 128x128 JPG or PNG)
  • Short description of the publication e.g. "Betty Bossi is the leading cooking magazine in Switzerland"
  • URL of the publication website e.g. http://www.bettybossi.ch
  • Email addresses of the users of the Shortcut Manager (typically 1-3 users per publication)
  • Twitter name and Facebook address of the publication if available
